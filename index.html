The Album: "Couldn't Stand the Weather"

About the Album

"Couldn't Stand the Weather" was released on May 15, 1984 by Epic Records. The album featured Double Trouble with Stevie Ray Vaughan on guitar and vocals, Tommy Shannon on bass, and Chris Layton on drums.

Additional personnel included Stevie's brother Jimmie Vaughan on rhythm guitar for "Couldn't Stand the Weather" and "The Things That I Used to Do." Fran Christina played drums and Stan Harrison played tenor saxophone on "Stang's Swang."

The album was produced by Stevie Ray Vaughan and Double Trouble, Richard Mullen, and Jim Capfer, with executive producer John H. Hammond. Richard Mullen was principal Engineer, and Rob Eaton was Assistant Engineer. Cover art was created by Holland MacDonald, with Benno Friedman providing Photography.
